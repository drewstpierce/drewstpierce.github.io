ABOUT

Drew Pierce is an interactive content developer from Knoxville, Tennessee specializing in video games and other amusements made with HTML5 and sequential 2D animation. This isn't to say Drew's sole focus as a designer/developer is HTML5. He also develops off the browser with the C languages and their more UI-centric libraries (SDL and Ncurses, as of late), usually in a Unix'y environment with Emacs or Sublime Text. He's even capable of making a website for whatever thing you might be trying to sell (especially if you're going for an old programming manual aesthetic).

With a BA in English literature and an attention to detail that most psychiatric specialists would classify as apophenia, Drew's projects often find their roots in some narrative concept or research-spawned subtextual analysis that he probably won't speak about unless asked.

Current projects include Dopesmoker: The Game of the Album by Sleep, the Band, the current version of which is being developed with Phaser (the JavaScript framework), and Spiritual Enterprise, a digital echo chamber for cult leaders wanting to bring their disheveled interpretations of the unknowable straight to the internet, as God probably never intended.
